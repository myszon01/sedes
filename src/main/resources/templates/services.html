Services

Less than Load Trucking (LTL)

Less than Load Trucking (LTL) allows you to ship your freight and only pay for the space you use. This service keeps your logistics needs lower in price, while ensuring that you receive the best American Freight Logistics customer services. We have a large network of terminals so we can guarantee that your regional and long-haul needs are met on budget and on time. It’s part of how we can achieve a 98% on-time performance level.

Full Truckload (TL)

Full Truckload (TL) services all you to ship larger loads of products that require to be transported using the entire truck. We are able to delivery our customers' shipments throughout the continental United States. We are also able to do cross dock shipments. We make it our priority to make sure all your loads are handled in a concise and efficient way, because we realize that you are entrusting us with the very essential responsibility of transporting your shipment.

Cross-dock/Warehouse

We can handle your warehouse needs, such as cross-dock, to give you the most efficient and budget-conscious logistics experience.
